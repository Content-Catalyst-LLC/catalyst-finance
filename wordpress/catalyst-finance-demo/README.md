# Catalyst Finance Workspace v2.0.0

Shortcodes:

- `[catalyst_finance_workspace]`
- `[catalyst_finance_demo]`
- `[catalyst_finance_demo mode="public"]`

The browser module contains nine contract-driven studios: annual screening, capital budgeting, alternative comparison, uncertainty and stress testing, pricing and elasticity, cost and operating economics, sustainable finance, governance and publication, and the connected financial decision intelligence platform. Browser data remains local until it is exported or intentionally handed to another product.

## Connected Platform Studio

The v2.0.0 platform surface registers Sustainable Catalyst products and capabilities; links immutable finance artifacts to decision cases; validates dependency order; calculates portfolio value without derivative double counting; evaluates case readiness and product health; and enforces governance and information-classification rules on cross-product handoffs.

The sample platform publication reports portfolio NPV, adjusted NPV, capital requirements, annual value, confidence, downside exposure, risk-adjusted value, case status, handoff completion, rejected transfers, and integration warnings. Its downloadable integration manifest preserves model, contract, workspace, scenario, revision, checksum, classification, and governance identifiers.

## Governance and publication

The governance studio preserves claim traceability, review findings, approvals, audit records, redaction, public JSON, and Decision Studio / Knowledge Library handoff manifests. The connected platform consumes those governed records without weakening their publication controls.

## Product boundary

Catalyst Finance supports structured analysis and human-governed coordination. It does not autonomously approve investments, lending, pricing, procurement, funding, accounting treatment, assurance, or fiduciary decisions.

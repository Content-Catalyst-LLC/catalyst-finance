# Catalyst Finance v1.6.0

## Demand, Elasticity, Pricing, and Revenue Studio

This release promotes the former compatibility elasticity utility into a separate canonical pricing model without changing its legacy command behavior.

### Added

- Typed pricing definition and publication contracts.
- Linear, constant-elasticity, and observed demand curves.
- Multi-segment demand aggregation and proportional capacity allocation.
- Revenue, contribution, and operating-profit optimization.
- Point/local elasticity classifications, contribution margins, and break-even quantities.
- Current-price deltas and maximum price-change constraints.
- Pricing CLI, FastAPI route, JSON/CSV examples, and generated schemas.
- Versioned pricing analyses in persistent workspaces.
- Exact Python/JavaScript parity and an interactive WordPress pricing studio.
- v1.5.0 migration support across screening, cash-flow, comparison, uncertainty, and workspace records.

### Boundary

Catalyst Finance remains decision-support software. Pricing decisions require human review of evidence, fairness, accessibility, contracts, taxes, regulation, competition, and market response.

# Catalyst Finance v1.8.0 — Sustainable Finance, Carbon, and Natural-Capital Accounting

## Added

- Canonical sustainable-finance contract and shared Python/JavaScript engine.
- Avoided-emissions, shadow-price, and market-credit accounting with a non-double-count policy.
- Natural-capital stock uplift, confidence adjustment, ecosystem-service present value, and restoration cost.
- Probability-adjusted transition benefits and costs.
- Green-financing interest-rate savings.
- Adjusted project NPV and disclosed component reconciliation.
- JSON and CSV publication exports.
- FastAPI, CLI, workspace revision, migration, and WordPress studio support.

## Compatibility

All v1.7.0 finance contracts and workspace exports migrate to v1.8.0. Existing screening, cash-flow, comparison, uncertainty, pricing, and operating engines remain available.

# Catalyst Finance v2.0.0

## Connected Financial Decision Intelligence Platform

Catalyst Finance v2.0.0 introduces the ninth canonical engine and completes the v1 roadmap. The release connects versioned finance, evidence, computation, intelligence, and decision artifacts without flattening their provenance or governance boundaries.

### Added

- connected-product registry with versions, roles, supported contracts, status, and classification policies;
- immutable cross-product artifact references with revision identity and SHA-256 checksums;
- portfolio aggregation with explicit derivative-artifact exclusion;
- risk-adjusted value, confidence, downside, capital, and annual-value summaries;
- multi-artifact decision cases and readiness scoring;
- acyclic dependency graph validation and deterministic processing order;
- governed handoff envelopes with classification, approval, status, destination, and payload-hash controls;
- product health, integration manifests, reproducible run records, and institutional flags;
- platform CLI, FastAPI endpoint, persistent workspace revisions, schemas, fixtures, and exact browser parity;
- WordPress Financial Decision Intelligence Platform studio;
- complete v1.9.0 migration support for all existing contracts and workspace records.

### Platform boundary

The platform coordinates artifacts and review state. It does not grant access to connected systems, replace their authorization controls, transmit credentials, certify accounting treatment, provide assurance, or make decisions autonomously.

# Catalyst Finance v1.3.0 — Cash-Flow Modeling and Capital Budgeting

Released: 2026-07-17

## Purpose

Replace the annual-benefit screening shortcut with a transparent period-by-period cash-flow engine while preserving the v1.2.0 workspace and screening model.

## Delivered

- Monthly, quarterly, and annual period models.
- Initial and phased capital costs; recurring and irregular operating costs; revenue, savings, avoided costs, grants, rebates, residual value, decommissioning, working capital, and working-capital recovery.
- Nominal/real basis validation and effective annual-to-period rate conversion.
- Recurring schedules with intervals and annual escalation.
- NPV, simple payback, discounted payback, IRR root detection, MIRR, profitability index, benefit-cost ratio, equivalent annual value, cumulative cash flow, and terminal value.
- Explicit metric traces listing included and excluded categories and flow identifiers.
- Conventional, irregular, negative, zero-cost, and multiple-sign-change benchmark fixtures.
- Cash-flow CLI, FastAPI route, JSON/Markdown/CSV exports, workspace revision support, JavaScript parity engine, and WordPress capital-budgeting studio.
- v1.2.0 screening migration alongside existing v1.0.0 and v1.1.0 migration paths.

## Acceptance evidence

- Period tables reconcile to aggregate undiscounted and discounted results.
- Irregular quarterly and monthly fixtures validate.
- Multiple-sign-change cases list all detected roots and suppress a misleading single IRR.
- Nominal/real mismatches fail contract validation.
- Every reported metric carries a trace to included cash-flow categories and source flow IDs.
- Python and browser results match exactly for all five capital-budgeting fixtures.

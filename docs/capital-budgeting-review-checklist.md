# Capital-Budgeting Review Checklist

Before relying on a Catalyst Finance cash-flow result, confirm:

1. The analysis horizon covers implementation, operations, and closure.
2. Capital spending is placed in the periods when it occurs.
3. Recurring and irregular costs and benefits are modeled separately.
4. Nominal cash flows use a nominal discount rate, or real cash flows use a real discount rate.
5. Escalation assumptions are disclosed and not double-counted with inflation.
6. Working capital and its recovery are both represented where applicable.
7. Residual value and decommissioning are supported by evidence.
8. IRR is not treated as decisive when multiple sign changes are present.
9. NPV, MIRR, benefit-cost ratio, and cumulative cash-flow behavior are reviewed together.
10. Metric traces and excluded categories are examined before publication.

Catalyst Finance is educational decision-support software, not financial, investment, tax, accounting, legal, fiduciary, procurement, funding, lending, or assurance advice.

# Governance and Publication Review Checklist

## Artifact identity

- Confirm the workspace, scenario, artifact, and revision identifiers.
- Confirm the model identifier and version match the reviewed calculation.
- Confirm headline metrics exactly match the referenced artifact.

## Evidence

- Verify every headline claim links to a metric or calculation.
- Verify every headline claim links to assumptions, sources, and evidence.
- Check source dates, ownership, applicability, and review status.
- Confirm evidence locators are specific enough for another reviewer to reproduce the finding.

## Methodology and limitations

- Review methodology versions and model identifiers.
- Disclose data-quality issues, conflicts, limitations, and excluded factors.
- Confirm private or licensed sources are not exposed publicly.

## Review workflow

- Resolve every finding, objection, and revision requirement explicitly.
- Preserve prior review events rather than replacing them.
- Confirm approval comes from an authorized reviewer.
- Confirm publication and withdrawal events are recorded separately.

## Publication

- Validate the redacted public payload.
- Check the Markdown and accessible HTML brief.
- Verify the audit-chain head and run hashes.
- Review Knowledge Library and Decision Studio handoff identifiers.

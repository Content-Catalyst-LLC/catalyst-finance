# Sustainable Finance Review Checklist

- Confirm organizational and project emissions boundaries.
- Confirm baseline year, project case, units, and avoided-emissions calculation.
- Select one carbon valuation basis and verify no double counting.
- Confirm carbon-credit additionality, eligibility, ownership, permanence, leakage, and verification cost.
- Confirm natural-capital asset ownership, condition, quantity, unit, valuation source, and confidence.
- Review whether ecosystem-service flows overlap with project cash flows.
- Validate transition probabilities, timing, and discount rate.
- Confirm green-financing principal, rate differential, term, fees, and covenants.
- Reconcile total sustainable value to adjusted project NPV.
- Preserve source evidence and obtain accounting, legal, ecological, and assurance review before external reporting.

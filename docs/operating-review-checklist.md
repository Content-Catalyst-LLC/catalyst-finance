# Operating Economics Review Checklist

Before relying on a v1.9.0 operating publication, confirm:

- budget and actual units refer to the same output measure;
- prices and variable costs use the same currency and period basis;
- direct fixed costs and allocated overhead are not double counted;
- cost-center labels and allocation methods match the source records;
- favorable and unfavorable signs are interpreted using the disclosed positive-is-favorable policy;
- the four component variances reconcile to the operating-profit variance;
- break-even results are not used when contribution margin is non-positive;
- high operating leverage is reviewed as a sensitivity warning, not a forecast;
- target-profit volume is feasible within capacity and demand constraints; and
- the source workspace, scenario, and revision identifiers are retained.

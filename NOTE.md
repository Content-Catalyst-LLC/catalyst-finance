# Note

Catalyst Finance is part of the Sustainable Catalyst platform. Version 2.0.0 adds evidence ledgers, claim traceability, institutional review, approvals, append-only audit history, redaction, reproducible briefs, and governed publication handoffs while preserving all seven existing finance engines.

The software is educational decision support, not financial, investment, accounting, tax, legal, fiduciary, lending, procurement, publication-assurance, or governance advice.
